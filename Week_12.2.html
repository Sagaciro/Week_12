Escherichia coli strain detection and characterization on fecal samples

The method used to identify and characterize E. coli strains in fecal samples was taken and modified from PanPhlAn 3.

“PanPhlAn is a strain-level metagenomic profiling tool for identifying the gene composition of individual strains in metagenomic samples.”1 PanPhlAn is a tool for culture-free microbial population studies based on metagenomic data and it does not require genome assembly.
